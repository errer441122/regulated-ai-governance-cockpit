# EU AI Act Public Source Note

Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj?locale=en

Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 is a public legal source for the EU Artificial Intelligence Act. This local note does not provide legal advice; it extracts portfolio-review themes from the public regulation.

A high-risk AI system needs documented risk management, data governance, technical documentation, logging, transparency, human oversight, accuracy, robustness, cybersecurity, and post-market monitoring before it could be put into a regulated real-world context.

For this portfolio, the practical lesson is narrower: do not present a demo score, RAG answer, benchmark, or dashboard as an automated legal, credit, employment, eligibility, or public-benefit decision. Keep model outputs advisory and make human accountability visible.
