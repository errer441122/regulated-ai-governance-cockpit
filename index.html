        Follow-up workflow layer

        From Customer Signal to Follow-up Workflow

        This section demonstrates how customer signals from the cockpit could trigger owner assignment, alerts, and recurring follow-up routines through no-code or low-code tools.

        Why follow-up workflows matter for this profile

        The cockpit identifies customer signals such as recurring journey friction, low satisfaction, delayed follow-up, service complaints and low engagement. Follow-up workflows close the loop by turning those signals into assigned tasks, alerts, and scheduled reviews. This bridges the gap between analysis and execution - the core value of a customer experience analytics profile.
