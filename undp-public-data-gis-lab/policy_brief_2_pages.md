# Policy Brief

## Purpose

This lab shows how a small public-data workflow can connect SDG-style indicators, GIS-lite visualization, and responsible AI boundaries.

## Interpretation

The output is a prioritization brief for discussion. It must not be used for aid allocation, eligibility, procurement, sanctions, or automated program decisions.

## Review Boundary

Human review is required for interpretation. Local context, data freshness, and qualitative constraints matter more than the numeric score.
